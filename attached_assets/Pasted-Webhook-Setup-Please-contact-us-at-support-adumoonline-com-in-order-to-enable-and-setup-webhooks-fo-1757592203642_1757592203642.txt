Webhook Setup
Please contact us at support@adumoonline.com in order to enable and setup webhooks for your application.
You need to expose an endpoint in your system and send us the url in order for us to configure your webhook.
Adumo Online will send the results of each transaction to this endpoint up to a maximum of 3 times.
Once our system receives a 200 OK message we will stop sending the webhook.
Your system will need to interpret the below JSON payload, once received your system should return a 200 OK message.
The JSON payload will be sent via a HTTP POST, so your endpoint needs to accept a POST.
